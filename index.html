I'm a creative Web Developer based in the beautiful SF Bay Area. Programming and web designing intersect in a very unique way that empowers us with endless creative and practical possibilities. They connect us and propel us forward with shared ideas and experiences, and it's an exciting, ever-changing landscape to be in. I enjoy challenging myself with solutions that show beauty and simplicity without sacrificing functionality. The rewarding feeling that comes with helping and enabling others achieve their goals is the icing on the cake, and I fully embrace every opportunity to create amazing websites together.

In my spare time I enjoy local hikes along the peninsula. On weekends you can find me with my volunteer group (Encores on Wheels) jamming out music at various senior homes.
